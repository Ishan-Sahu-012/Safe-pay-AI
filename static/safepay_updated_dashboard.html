How SafePay AI Works

1

User Input

You scan a QR code, paste a UPI ID, or receive a suspicious SMS link.

2

Data Extraction

SafePay securely parses the payment details, URLs, or sender info locally.

3

AI Analysis

Data is sent to our FastAPI backend for deep machine learning analysis.

4

Fraud Detection Models

Models check patterns, known scam databases, and behavioral anomalies.

5

Risk Score Generation

A normalized risk score (0-100%) and confidence metric are generated.

6

Safety Recommendation & Alert

Instant alert is presented with specific reasons: "Safe to Pay" or "High Risk Scam".

How to Install SafePay AI Chrome Extension

1

Download

Download the SafePay AI extension package.

2

Extract

Extract the downloaded ZIP folder to a known location.

3

Open Chrome

Launch Google Chrome.

4

Navigate to Extensions

Type chrome://extensions in the address bar.

5

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle "Developer mode" in the top right corner.

6

Load Unpacked

Click the "Load unpacked" button.

7

Select Folder

Select the extracted SafePay AI extension folder.

8

Pin Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon and pin SafePay AI to your toolbar.

9

Verify

Open any website and click the icon to verify it's active.

Troubleshooting

If the extension icon doesn't appear, ensure you selected the correct folder containing the manifest.json file during Step 7. Check the Chrome extensions page for any error messages.

Cyber Awareness Hub

UPI Scams

Scammers send "Collect Requests". Entering your PIN deducts money instead of receiving it.

QR Scams

Fake QR codes pasted over real ones at shops, or sent via WhatsApp to "receive money".

OTP Scams

Tricking users into sharing OTPs under the guise of KYC updates or package deliveries.

Fake KYC

SMS claiming your bank account/SIM will be blocked unless you click a link to update KYC.

Customer Care

Fake toll-free numbers on Google search leading to scammers who ask for screen sharing apps.

Investment Scams

Telegram groups promising 200% returns in 24 hours for small crypto or UPI deposits.

Job Scams

Offers for "Work from home YouTube liking jobs" that eventually ask for an upfront "security deposit".

Social Engineering

Impersonating relatives or bosses in urgent need of money via WhatsApp.

Fraud Detection Education

Risk Score Explanation

Low Risk (0-30%)

Verified merchant, normal behavior.

Medium (31-74%)

New UPI ID or unusual text patterns. Verify before paying.

High Risk (75-100%)

Known scammer, malicious links, or urgent manipulative language.

We extract the underlying URI (e.g., upi://pay) from the image and check the VPA, payee name, and parameters against fraud databases.

Natural Language Processing models analyze urgency, intent, URL reputation, and keyword combinations to flag phishing.

  • Sense of extreme urgency (e.g., "Account Blocked in 2 hours").
  • Asking to scan QR to *receive* money.
  • Grammatical errors and misspelled bank names.
  • Unsolicited links from unknown numbers.
